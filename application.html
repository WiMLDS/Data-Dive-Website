Application

We ask people to give us a little information about themselves and their experience working with data, to make sure we have the right data heros at the data dive. We understand answering some of the questions requires some work. It's just, we are not the biggest fan of scales: one person's 3 could be another person's 5.

We will use the information you provide to make sure we have a good mix of skills on the day of the event, so you can learn something cool and show something cool. So, tell us a little about yourself and we look forward to hearing from you.

Application Fee: We will be asking everybody who we invite to join us on November 6th and 7th for a nominal fee of $5 to reserve their spot. We will donate all money collected to a charity which we'll vote on during the data dive.
